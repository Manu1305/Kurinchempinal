About Kurinchempinal family

Since 1984, the Kurinchempinal family temple has been a sacred place of worship and spiritual guidance. Our temple serves as a center for devotion, cultural preservation, and community gatherings for our extended family and devotees.

The temple is dedicated to preserving our ancestral traditions and providing a peaceful sanctuary for prayer, meditation, and religious ceremonies. We welcome all family members and devotees to join us in our spiritual journey.
